ArcGIS Online Web Map

I created an interactive story map on ArcGIS online for the County of San Luis Obispo's capital improvement projects. I used map services maintained and updated by the county. This project was to showcase the county's capital improvement projects to the public and fullfilled my final project requirement for my advanced GIS undergraduate course. Other Projects

During this internship, I worked on several projects. I mapped bridge data, storm locations, road easements, guard rails, county facilities, culverts, collision locations, and county monuments. I updated the county's index maps which including mapping tract maps, parcel maps, corner records, and records of survey. Additionally, I created map books in ArcMap showing manholes, electrical systems, and sewage systems. I worked with ArcPad to collect field data and worked with Trimple GPS units. I also geocoded business addresses and used the COGO tool in ArcMap to digitize AutoCAD drawings. I used ArcMap, aerial imagery, and record drawings to review, compile, and edit infrastructure data for the county's GIS database. My work involved GIS data compiliation, editing, and research.
