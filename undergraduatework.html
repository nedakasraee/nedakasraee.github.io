For my undergraduate research position, I was part of a team that was developing a semi-automated system for processing hyperspectral imagery from the Conring SHARK camera. This project was interdisciplinary and included students and faculty in the Aerospace Engineering Deparment, Agribusiness Program, and Animal Science Program at California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo. I also worked with engineers at Corning Corporation and Harris Geospatial Solutions. This work was largely exploratory, and I was tasked with early processing of the hyperspectral images. Using ENVI (software) and IDL, I found errors and limitations with the dataset. I also investigate whether there was a correlation between specific wavelengths and vine stem water potential.
